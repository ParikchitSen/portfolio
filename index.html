About

I'm a computer engineering student at Kathmandu University (batch of 2023), currently working through coursework in algorithms, computer graphics, databases, and software engineering. Alongside that, I build full projects end to end — from a hackathon-winning road safety tool to a reinforcement-learning traffic controller, a hospital management system, and a search engine built from scratch.

I recently moved my daily development environment from Windows to Ubuntu, and most evenings now involve some mix of GPU driver configuration, OpenGL debugging, and Neovim motions. When I'm not at a terminal, I'm usually tracking down an old film worth watching or reorganizing a music library.

SoilSense AI

A soil-scanning app with an AI advisor that runs offline

A React Native front end paired with a local retrieval-augmented generation backend. A user scans a soil sample and the app returns crop recommendations, treatment suggestions, and field advice, grounded in agricultural reference material from NARC, MoALD, and the FAO, served through Ollama running quantized Qwen2.5 and Gemma 2 models on a local GPU.

  • React Native
  • Ollama
  • RAG
